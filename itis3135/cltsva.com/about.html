About CLTSVA

Who We Are

A student led organization devoted to supporting our fellow Veterans, Active Military Personnel, ROTC Cadets, their families adn supporter attending UNC Charlotte. Creating a community to lean on and help support one another.

Mission

Our mission is to offer opportunities for members of the military community, fostering an inclusive environment for veterans and military families within the university.

Our Purpose

  • Create a social support network and bring a sense of community for veterans and military affiliated individuals.
  • Provide access to career development, educational opportunities, and services to aid in the successful transition from military to civilian life.
  • Be inclusive of all military-affiliated individuals including current and future military personnel, veterans, their spouses and families, and supporters.
