FAQ

What is the SVA-49ers at UNC Charlotte? ▼

  • SVA-49ers is UNC Charlotte’s chapter of Student Veterans of America, focused on community building among student veterans, military families, and allies.
  • They host advocacy efforts, networking events, social gatherings, and academic support.
  • You can join through Niner Engage (the student organization platform).

What is MVS at UNC Charlotte? ▼

  • MVS stands for Military & Veteran Services — the official university office for military-affiliated students.
  • They support active-duty, National Guard, Reserve, veterans, and their families.
  • They are located in Barnard 103.
