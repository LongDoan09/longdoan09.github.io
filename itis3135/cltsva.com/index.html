Upcoming UNC System SVA Tournament

We’re thrilled to invite you to the Inaugural UNC System SVA Tournament hosted at the 49th Acre Tailgating Pavilion at UNC Charlotte! This special event is designed to foster networking, camaraderie, and collaboration among students, veterans, and community members.

Event Details

  • Location: 49th Acre Tailgating Pavilion, UNC Charlotte
  • Set-Up Begins: 10:00 AM
  • Programming: 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM

Expect tournament-style field day games, meaningful conversations, and opportunities to share best practices for engaging students within your SVA chapters.

Please bring your own tent (university colors/branding preferred!) and lawn chairs. Lunch will be provided.

Additional Information

After registering, please send your chapter or school logo for inclusion on the official event flyer. We also encourage you to share the event on your social media channels!
